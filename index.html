About Hotel

Check our story

Hotel Aurora combines all the best of an enchanting fell cottage with carefree, quality hotel accommodation. All our rooms come with their own sauna, and some rooms have a fireplace. We are located right in the centre of Luosto, in the immediate vicinity of all services and events. Hotel Aurora is themed on the Northern Lights, which you can see in our restaurant décor and our Northern Lights alarm system. This small, idyllic hotel is the perfect place for relaxed and exciting active holidays. Our hotel also provides an excellent setting for weddings or company meetings.
